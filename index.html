Thailand Adventures: Winter in Loei

Schtieve on Oct 26, 2025

Loei / Chiang Khan



Loei is a sparsely populated, mountainous province located in the northeastern part of Thailand known as Isan.

Chiang Khan is a charming riverside town found along the Mekong River across from Laos. It’s known for its well-preserved wooden houses, relaxed atmosphere, and rich local culture.

The town’s main street, Chiang Khan Walking Street (Thanon Chai Khong), comes alive in the evenings with traditional food stalls, crafts, and local souvenirs.

Visitors often enjoy biking along the riverfront, giving alms to monks in the early morning, and watching sunsets over the Mekong.

Chiang Khan blends nostalgic Thai village life with a touch of modern tourism, making it a favorite for those seeking a peaceful, scenic getaway.
